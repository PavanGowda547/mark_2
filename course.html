PU

Vijaya composite PU College was founded in the year 1998 by the doyen of education in India, sri k.s Chandrashekaraiah, it is a continuation of The Legacy of Vijaya High School. Initially it started with just three sections with the PCMB combinations. Later, sections for Electronics And Computer Science where added. The infrastructure of the college such as library and the science Laboratories was always state-of-the-art.

Degree

The inauguration of VEIT Degree College and the commencement of I semester BCA classed are significant milestones in the illustrious history of Vijaya Education Institute Trust. Established in 1956, Vijaya Education Institute Trust has been instrumental in providing quality education to the students at different levels from Nursery to Pre-university. VEIT Degree college which starts functioning today, is yet another addition to the impressive list of institutions under VEIT.

Innovation Lab

The lab was inaugurated by Sri U B Pravin Rao, the present COO for Infosys. Vijaya Education Institute Trust, known for its quality education and academic excellence aims to shape the achievers of future and equip them with robust knowledge, strong intrinsic values, state of the art study skills and top class capabilities. The college offers best of infrastructure, excellent library and laboratory and an enabling environment for learning.

Seminar Hall

This hall has an LCD projector and a computer system which the students use to present their seminar papers. Guest Lectures are also conducted frequently here, for improving vision of staff and students. 300 people can be seated here.

Library

The spacious, well-equipped library with the required ambience to motivate both students and teachers has a collection of 16,000 books, reference volumes, encyclopedias, dictionaries etc. A separate library for the students of BCA has been established. One of the distinguishing features of this library, the thoughtful creation of a separate well furnished airy corridor with the requisite facilities for the BCA students. This is expected to become a learning hub for those seeking knowledge.
